## 🎤 ABOUT THE Drinkers

**The Drinkers** are a **Slovenian booze rock band** from **Litija, Slovenia**.

### Formation Story:
On a warm summer night in **July 1993** in Litija, along drinking pints of cold beer, The Drinkers were established. Five friends, all of them former musicians in different bands, came up with the idea to form a band, able to perform on a local rock festival called **"Rusty Trumpets"** (Rjave Trobente).

Said and done. By surprise they even **won on that festival** and afterwards decided to stay together and perform as a band even in the future.

In **1994** a new bass guitarist Miro M. joined the band.
