## 💡 PRO TIPS

### **Maximize Sales:**

```
1. Create urgency (limited quantity + time)
2. Show progress (X% sold)
3. Use FOMO (fear of missing out)
4. Post at peak times (8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 6-9 PM)
5. Engage with every comment
6. Share to all platforms
7. Email your list multiple times
8. Use countdown timers
```

### **Content Tips:**

```
1. Use high-quality images
2. Show product from all angles
3. Include lifestyle shots
4. Use consistent branding
5. Include clear CTAs
6. Make links easy to find
7. Use emojis strategically
8. Keep copy concise
```
