## 🎨 CREATION TOOLS

### Free Options:
1. **Canva** - Templates for reels
2. **CapCut** - Mobile video editing
3. **InShot** - Quick edits
4. **DaVinci Resolve** - Professional (free)

### AI Options:
1. **Runway ML** - AI video generation
2. **Pika Labs** - Text to video
3. **HeyGen** - Avatar videos

### Quick Workflow:
1. Use existing band photos
2. Add text overlays in Canva
3. Add music from YouTube Audio Library
4. Export as 9:16 vertical
5. Upload to Instagram/TikTok
