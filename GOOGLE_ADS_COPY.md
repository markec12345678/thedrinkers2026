### **GOOGLE ADS BEST PRACTICES:**

```
✅ Headlines: 30 characters max (including spaces)
✅ Descriptions: 90 characters max (including spaces)
✅ Use all 3 headlines (Google rotates)
✅ Use both descriptions
✅ Include keywords in headlines
✅ Include benefits, not just features
✅ Strong, action-oriented CTA
✅ Numbers & specifics > vague claims
```
