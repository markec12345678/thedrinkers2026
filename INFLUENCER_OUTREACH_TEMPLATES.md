### **✅ DOBRO:**

### **⚠️ ZA IZBOLJŠATI:**
- ⚠️ Subject line predolg (cut off na mobile)
- ⚠️ "Smo veliki oboževalci" = generic flattery
- ⚠️ Ni social proof (zakaj bi jim bilo mar?)
- ⚠️ Ni specificnega why them (feels mass sent)
- ⚠️ "Kaj pričakujemo" = sounds demanding
- ⚠️ Ni urgency ali scarcity
- ⚠️ CTA prešibek ("Si zainteresiran?")
- ⚠️ Ni follow-up sequence

## ✨ DRASTIČNO IZBOLJŠANE VERZIJE

### **BEST PRACTICES ZA INFLUENCER OUTREACH:**

```
✅ Subject: <40 characters (mobile optimization)
✅ Personalization: Specific compliment (not generic)
✅ Social Proof: Why you're credible
✅ Value First: What THEY get (before what you get)
✅ Low Friction: Easy yes (not big commitment)
✅ Clear CTA: Specific next step
✅ Follow-up: 3-4 messages planned
```

## 🎯 BEST PRACTICES

### **Subject Lines:**
```
✅ Personaliziraj z [IME]
✅ Uporabi emoji (1 max)
✅ Bodi specifičen (ne generic "Sodelovanje")
✅ Ustvari curiosity (vprašanje deluje)
✅ <40 characters (mobile optimization)
```

### **Email Body:**
```
✅ Specific compliment (ne "veliki oboževalci")
✅ Social proof (številke, achievements)
✅ Value first (oni dobijo več kot ti)
✅ Low friction (easy yes)
✅ Clear CTA (specific next step)
✅ P.S. z urgency/scarcity
```

### **Follow-up:**
```
✅ 3-4 follow-ups (večina odgovori na 2.-3.)
✅ Add value vs "checking in"
✅ Easy opt-out ("ni časa, razumem")
✅ Urgency/scarcity (limited spots)
✅ Breakup email (zadnji klic)
```
