## 🎯 BEST PRACTICES

### **Subject Lines:**
- ✅ Uporabi emoji (🍺, 🎸, 🎫)
- ✅ Personaliziraj z [IME]
- ✅ Ustvari urgency (⏰, Zadnja priložnost)
- ✅ Bodi specifičen (FREE album, 30% popust)

### **Body Copy:**
- ✅ Kratki odstavki (1-3 stavki)
- ✅ Clear CTA (en glavni link)
- ✅ Benefits over features
- ✅ Social proof (številke, limiti)

### **Timing:**
- ✅ Torek-Sreda 10:00 (najboljši open rate)
- ✅ Četrtek 14:00 (dober za engagement)
- ❌ Ponedeljek (preveč inboxov)
- ❌ Petek popoldan (ljudje odhajajo)

### **Segmentation:**
```
New VIPs (0-30 days):
→ Welcome serija (3 emaili)

Active VIPs:
→ Weekly engagement

Inactive VIPs (60+ days):
→ Re-engagement campaign

VIPs who bought tickets:
→ Post-concert follow-up
```
