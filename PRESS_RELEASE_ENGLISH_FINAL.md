### ℹ️ ABOUT THE BAND

**The Drinkers** are a legendary Slovenian rock band founded in **1993 in Litija**. With over **30 years of career**, **7 studio albums**, and **hundreds of concerts** across Slovenia and the region, they are one of the most recognizable and beloved rock bands in the country.

Best known for their energetic live performances and the cult anthem **"Pijemo ga radi"** (We Love to Drink), The Drinkers have become synonymous with Slovenian rock'n'roll. Their concerts are legendary for their unforgettable atmosphere and fan interaction.

**Discography Highlights:**
