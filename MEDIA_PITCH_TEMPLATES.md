### **✅ DOBRO:**
- ✅ "EXCLUSIVE" v subjectu (attention grabber)
- ✅ Clear value proposition
- ✅ Bullet points za features
- ✅ Press kit mentioned
- ✅ Exclusive offer za bralce
- ✅ CTA included

### **⚠️ ZA IZBOLJŠATI:**
- ⚠️ Subject predolg (cut off na mobile)
- ⚠️ "Digitalna revolucija" = buzzword, vague
- ⚠️ Ni social proof (zakaj bi jim bilo mar?)
- ⚠️ Ni specificnega why this outlet (feels mass sent)
- ⚠️ Features over benefits (kaj bralci dobijo?)
- ⚠️ "Zainteresirani za cover story?" = preveč, ni za cover
- ⚠️ Ni follow-up sequence
- ⚠️ Ni novinarjevega imena personalizacije

## ✨ DRASTIČNO IZBOLJŠANE VERZIJE

### **BEST PRACTICES ZA MEDIA PITCH:**

```
✅ Subject: <50 characters (mobile optimization)
✅ Personalization: Novinarjevo ime + outlet specifično
✅ Hook: Zakaj je to zgodba ZA NJIHOVE bralce
✅ Social Proof: Zakaj je to pomembno (numbers, firsts)
✅ Benefits: Kaj bralci dobijo (ne samo features)
✅ Low Friction: Easy yes (press kit ready)
✅ Clear CTA: Specific next step
✅ Follow-up: 2-3 messages planned
```

## 🎯 BEST PRACTICES

### **Subject Lines:**
```
✅ Personaliziraj z [OUTLET] ali [IME]
✅ Uporabi 1 emoji max
✅ Bodi specifičen (ne generic "EXCLUSIVE")
✅ <50 characters (mobile optimization)
✅ Ustvari curiosity (zakaj je to zanje)
```

### **Email Body:**
```
✅ Specific compliment (raziskuj novinarja)
✅ Social proof (številke, achievements)
✅ Benefits za bralce (ne samo features)
✅ Press kit ready (low friction)
✅ Clear CTA (specific next step)
✅ P.S. z urgency/scarcity
```

### **Follow-up:**
```
✅ 2-3 follow-ups (večina odgovori na 2.)
✅ Add value vs "checking in"
✅ Easy opt-out ("ni časa, razumem")
✅ Urgency/scarcity (limited interview slots)
✅ Breakup email (zadnji klic)
```
